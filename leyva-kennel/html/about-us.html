About Us

Meet Us

Our Story

Leyva Kennels was born from a genuine passion for animals—especially for the American Bully XL. Ever since I was a child, I’ve had a deep connection with animals of all kinds: dogs, cats, horses, snakes… But dogs have always been my favorite. Over six years ago, I became fascinated with this incredible breed and dedicated myself to learning everything about it: types, health, genetics, and care. After two years of searching and studying, I found Hera, our first American Bully, from the Giant Bully Pitbulls bloodline— and I instantly knew she was the one. Later came Kuba, and since then, this dream has only grown stronger.

A Family Project

Leyva Kennels is a family-run project. My parents and I share a deep love for animals, and we each contribute in our own way to make sure our dogs are always happy, healthy, and loved. My father, although no longer practicing, is a trained veterinarian— his knowledge and experience are essential to our daily routines and decisions.

How Our Dogs Live

Our dogs live with us in our home, in a peaceful countryside setting with access to open fields and a garden. They go out daily to run, play, and explore. Hera loves running alongside the bike, while Kuba prefers a more relaxed stroll, sniffing everything along the way. They’re raised around children and other animals, and socialized from day one. We don’t keep dogs in cages—we raise them in a loving family environment.

Breeding Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple: quality over quantity. We don’t breed just to breed. We can go years without a litter if the conditions aren’t right. Every pairing is carefully studied, prioritizing health, structure, and temperament. We aim to create our own healthy bloodline—massive but standard-compliant dogs that are noble, loyal, and adaptable. Perfect for both family life and protection work.

Our Vision

We want our clients to feel what we feel—to fall in love with this breed and become as passionate about it as we are. Our dream is to grow responsibly, to add new dogs, participate in shows, and eventually become certified professional breeders, especially in the K9 field. Everything we do, we do with love and purpose—for our dogs, for our clients, and for the future of the breed.
